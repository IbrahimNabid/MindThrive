MindThrive: Your Mental Health Oasis

Our Mission

Mental health, a crucial aspect of our overall well-being, is often neglected and ignored in society. It's estimated that 1 in 5 U.S. adults experience mental illness each year, and 1 in 6 U.S. youth experience a mental health disorder each year. Despite these statistics, mental health remains a stigmatized and misunderstood topic, especially in many communities of color.

MindThrive is a website to break down these barriers of stigma and misunderstanding that surround mental health. This website's goal is to provide a safe space, resources, and information to empower individuals on their mental wellness journey.
